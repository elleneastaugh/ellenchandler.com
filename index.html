About Me

I'm a strategist who believes great advertising starts with curiosity, not assumptions.

With 10 years in communications, I've worked across agencies like TBWA\Chiat\Day NY, RAPP, and Arnold, helping brands find their voice and their audience. I'm driven by the hunt for insights that unlock possibilities—the kind that make creative teams lean in and say "yes, and..."

I believe in strategies that are as elegant as they are effective. Original thinking that's true to the brand, relevant to real humans, and impossible to ignore.
