How I Work

I'm a strategist who believes the best work lives at the intersection of rigor and romance.

In college, I majored in both cognitive science and film production, which tells you 99% of what you need to know. I blend science with storytelling, data with empathy, and the business case with the case for big swings.

I've worked in industries including travel (Hilton, Southwest), insurance (Progressive, Farmers), automotive (Toyota, Lexus, Mazda), personal care (Philips Norleco, Sonicare, Avent), luxury (Waldorf Astoria, DeBeers) and dining (Habit Burger, Sierra Gold). I've won pitches, built platforms, run research, and written briefs that unlock the kind of work that wins Effies and moves inventory.

I do the geeky stuff and the cool stuff too, always coming back to why we're here and who it's for.

Because strategy without rigor is just opinion. And rigor without romance is just a really dry presentation.
